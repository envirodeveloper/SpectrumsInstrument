Emission Control for UAE Industries

Our Continuous Emission Monitoring System (CEMS) is engineered to meet the stringent regulatory requirements of the UAE's Ministry of Climate Change and Environment (MOCCAE) and key environmental zones such as Dubai Municipality, Abu Dhabi Department of Energy, and Sharjah Environment and Protected Areas Authority.

Ideal for power plants, cement factories, refineries, and industrial zones across the Emirates, this system provides real-time emission monitoring, stack analysis, and cloud-based reporting. CEMS ensures environmental compliance while helping UAE industries contribute to the nation's sustainability goals and net-zero commitments.

Why CEMS Matters in the UAE

  • Meets MOCCAE and GCC environmental emission norms
  • Delivers real-time, continuous stack emission data across UAE industries
  • Reduces compliance risks with automated online reporting to UAE regulators
  • Detects emission exceedances instantly for preventive action
  • Enhances transparency for ESG reporting in free zones and industrial cities
  • Helps meet EHS and sustainability KPIs across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and beyond
  • Seamless integration with SCADA systems and industrial IoT platforms
  • Supports predictive maintenance of scrubbers, filters, and control equipment
  • Cloud access for compliance officers, auditors, and plant managers
  • Contributes to UAE Net Zero 2050 vision through better emissions control
