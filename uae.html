Innovative Measurement Technologies for the UAE Market

Our instruments incorporate globally certified technologies adapted for UAE standards, helping you meet local compliance and quality benchmarks with confidence.

UV Spectroscopy

Principle: Measures UV absorbance between 200–800 nm to assess water quality in real-time.

UAE Applications:

  • Desalination and wastewater reuse (ADSSC, DEWA)
  • COD/TOC analysis in industrial zones (KIZAD, DIC)
  • Color monitoring in municipal treatment plants

Advantages: Fast, non-invasive, and maintenance-friendly design.

High-Temperature Oxidation

Principle: Combustion above 600°C to detect TOC, TN, and COD accurately.

UAE Applications:

  • Industrial effluent monitoring (ADNOC, EMAAR)
  • Environmental compliance in Sharjah & Ajman
  • Real-time detection in chemical and manufacturing hubs

Advantages: High precision in extreme process conditions.
