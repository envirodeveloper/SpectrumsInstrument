Smart Technologies for Precision Monitoring

Explore our powerful measurement methods—designed for accuracy, speed, and environmental compliance.

UV Technology

Principle: Uses UV spectroscopy (200–800 nm) to measure light absorbance in liquids.

Applications:

  • COD & TOC Measurement
  • Organic Compounds
  • Color Monitoring

Advantages: Fast, non-invasive, and low-maintenance operation.

High-Temperature Method

Principle: Sample combustion above 600°C to detect TOC, TN, and COD.

Applications:

  • Industrial Effluent Analysis
  • Environmental Testing
  • Wastewater Treatment

Advantages: Reliable, precise readings even in harsh conditions.
