Ambient Air

Quality Monitoring System

Solution Insight

Our Ambient Air Quality Monitoring System (AAQMS) provides continuous, real-time assessment of air pollution levels in industrial and urban areas. Designed to comply with national and international standards, the system offers high-precision data to support environmental compliance, public health, and regulatory reporting. With advanced sensors and cloud integration, our AAQMS helps you track pollutants such as PM2.5, PM10, NOx, SO2, CO, and O3 for smarter air quality management.

Why AAQMS Matters

  • Ensures compliance with CPCB/EPCA air quality guidelines
  • Provides real-time data on harmful pollutants like PM2.5, NOx, and SO2
  • Supports data-driven policy and urban planning decisions
  • Helps industries and municipalities monitor ambient air continuously
  • Improves public awareness and health response strategies
  • Enables predictive alerts and environmental risk assessments
  • Integrates seamlessly with IoT and smart city dashboards
  • Offers centralized cloud data access and reporting
  • Reduces manual sampling and increases data accuracy
  • Drives corporate environmental responsibility and transparency
