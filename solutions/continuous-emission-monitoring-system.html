Continuous Emission

Monitoring System (CEMS)

Solution Insight

Our Continuous Emission Monitoring System (CEMS) is a robust, regulatory-compliant solution designed to monitor and report emissions from industrial stacks in real time. Ideal for power plants, cement, chemical, and manufacturing units, CEMS helps industries maintain air quality compliance with CPCB/SPCB standards. With advanced sensor integration, data logging, and remote accessibility, it ensures accurate emissions tracking, environmental responsibility, and operational transparency.

Why CEMS Matters

  • Ensures compliance with environmental emission norms
  • Provides continuous, accurate stack emission data
  • Reduces regulatory risks and fines
  • Supports reporting to CPCB/SPCB through cloud dashboards
  • Detects emission spikes and equipment anomalies in real time
  • Improves accountability in high-pollution industries
  • Enables proactive maintenance of pollution control systems
  • Integrates with SCADA and plant automation
  • Supports historical data analysis and audit trail
  • Promotes sustainable and transparent industrial operations
